About the App!

This is an simple Number Facts app based on NumbersApi.com that provides facts under 4 categories (Trivia, Math, Date, Year). To use this app you first you have to select one type and type a number, date or year according to your selected type. Then the app will fetch the facts from numbersapi.com and display them to you in user friendly way.
